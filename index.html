I Truly enjoy the sport of volleyball and hope that I can make a difference in the community. My goal as an assistant coach is to make sure that the kids are learning the proper technique as well as enjoying the sport while they are still young. With my experience in the volleyball community, I hope to set a good example of what a good coach is like. Taking on this position of teaching kids from ages 14-15 has really opened my eyes.
